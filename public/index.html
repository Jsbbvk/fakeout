Rules

All players will be given the same question or statement.

Each player will create a lie that seems most reasonable.

Afterwards, all the lies (and one truth) is put into a pool of answers where everyone then has to pick which answer they believe is true. (You can't pick your own).

If you get people to pick your lie, you get 1 point for each player.
If you guess the truth you get 2 points.

Afterwards, players vote for the best / funniest lie. Player with the most votes gets 2 points.

Round then restarts.
